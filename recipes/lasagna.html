Lasagna

A pasta dish of layered pasta cheese and sauce

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozarella

Steps

  1. Lay a cooked pasta sheet in baking dish
  2. Spread mozarella and ricotta on top of sheet
  3. Layer tomato sauce on cheese
  4. Repeat steps 1 through 3 until baking dish is full or ingredients used up
